Aluminum or Aluminium?

What is the standard, "Aluminum" or "Aluminium"? In game it is "Aluminum", but we have a lot of "Aluminium" pages, links and images here. I like to use the game as standard and people prefer to say "Aluminum", but I also know "Aluminium" is the standard international name (ac. IUPAC). --Ezer'Arch (talk) 07:57, 13 November 2014 (EST)

VBAR template is squeezing pages

The new wiki theme brings a narrow article width, forcing text, images and tables to distribute vertically. The VBAR template squeezes the articles even more. Compare: Mars(with VBAR) and Galacticraft 4 (without VBAR). I propose to move the VBAR from Item template et al to beneath the Contents (on the right), where there's available blank space. --Ezer'Arch (talk) 20:37, 18 June 2017 (UTC)
