    Year and day length

    According to Minecraft.

    1 Day:

    • (Real life)/72
    • 1 RL minute = 1200 MC tics

    1 year, based on Moon cycle:

    • (Real life)/3.5
    Planet Earth days Minecraft minutes Proposal in minutes Proposal in Overworld days
    Sun 35.0 700 (11.66h) 700 (11.66h) 35
    Mercury 58.64 1172 (58.6h) 1200 (20h) 60
    Venus –243.0187 -4860 (81h) -600 (10h) -30
    Earth/Overworld 1.0 20 20 1
    Moon 27.32 160 160 8.0 (tidal locked)
    Mars 1.025 20 20 1
    Ceres 0.37809 8 10 1/2
    Jupiter 0.41007 8 10 1/2
    Saturn 0.426 9 10 1/2
    Uranus −0.71833 -14 -15 -3/4
    Neptune 0.67125 13 15 3/4
    Pluto −6.38718 -128 -120 -6
    Eris 1.08 22 20 1
    Haumea 0.163145 3 5 1/4

    Proposal values are rounded values so that planets' day-night cycles match with Overword's periodically.

    Negative values mean retrograde rotation, that is the Sun rises West and sets East.
