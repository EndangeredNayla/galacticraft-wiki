User: Radfast

From Galacticraft Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Co-developer of the Galacticraft mod, working with micdoodle8 since April 2014.

Special interests:

  • Physics and Space Science
  • Advanced machinery
  • Cool visual effects
  • Performance and lag reduction

List of radfast's main additions to Galacticraft 3:

  • Spinning space stations
  • freefall motion in space
  • Asteroids lighting
  • Astro Miner (the awesome model for it was made by Space Viking)
  • Arc Lamp
  • Display Screen
  • Telemetry Unit
  • All the gas machines and the Electric Arc Furnace and other Tier 2 Machines
  • minable Asteroid cores
  • All the new Gases in Galacticraft
  • Rewritten energy system
  • fixing sealed spaces!
  • Constant work on compatibility with other mods


List of radfast's main additions to Galacticraft 4:

  • Abandoned Base
  • Vacuum Glass
  • Lighting Panel
  • Hydraulic Platform
  • Magnetic Crafting Table
  • Switchable Aluminum Wire
  • Dark Decoration Block
  • Deconstructor
  • Chromatic Applicator
  • Space Emergency Kit and Emergency Post
  • Dungeon Locator
  • Strange Seed
  • oxygen leak detection using Sensor Glasses
  • dispensers placing rockets on Rocket Launch Pads
  • lots of small 'quality of life' changes
