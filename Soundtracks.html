Soundtracks

From Galacticraft Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search
This article is a stub. You can help us by expanding it

Galacticraft has its own in-game music, which complements the normal music in vanilla Minecraft. This music only plays in space dimensions.

In Galacticraft 3 Beta, a known issue is that the Galacticraft music is not working, that will be fixed in future.

As of Galacticraft 4, the music is working again!

Authorship

According to the Galacticraft website, the music was made by Jackson Cordes.
