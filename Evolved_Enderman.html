The Evolved Enderman is a that spawns in space dimensions.

They are slightly taller than their Overworld counterparts, and slightly harder to fight. They appear to be wearing two medium oxygen tanks, and oxygen gear and helmet. Just like their Overworld counterparts, they can teleport and have a chance of dropping ender pearls, and cannot be damaged with ranged weapons, as they will teleport away instead.

Like most of Minecraft hostile mobs, they will spawn in dimly lit areas except on Space Station and vanilla dimensions.
